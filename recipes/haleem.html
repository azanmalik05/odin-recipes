Haleem

Description

Haleem is a dish popular within South Asia. It is a type of stew that optionally includes wheat or barley, meat and lentils.

Ingredients

  • Wheat
  • Lentil
  • Beef
  • Barley
  • Silantro
  • Jalapeno

Steps

  1. Soak grains and lentils. Sauté onions, garlic, and ginger. Add the meat, sauté for a bit, then add the rest of the IP ingredients and pressure cook with the Meat/Stew Setting.
  2. Open the Pot. Remove the beef and give it just enough pulses in the food processor to shred. Then use an immersion blender to blend the lentils & grains.
  3. Add oats and cook the haleem down. Once it starts bubbling, cover and slow cook until you can see the laced consistency (or resha).
  4. Garnish and serve.
